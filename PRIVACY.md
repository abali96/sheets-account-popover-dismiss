# Privacy Policy

**Sheets Account Popover Dismiss** does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is tracked
- No data is sent to any server
- No analytics or telemetry
- No cookies or local storage used

The extension runs entirely locally in your browser. It only activates on Google Sheets pages (`docs.google.com/spreadsheets/*`) to dismiss the account popover dialog.

Last updated: January 2025
